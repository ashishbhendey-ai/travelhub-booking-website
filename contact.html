Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a hotel?

Booking a hotel on TravelHub is easy! Simply use our search tool to enter your destination, dates, and number of guests. Browse available options, compare prices, and click "Reserve" to complete your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with all booking details.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel most reservations through your account dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by hotel and booking type. Please review the specific cancellation policy for your booking before making changes. Contact our support team for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway to protect your financial information.

How do I track my booking?

You can track your booking by logging into your TravelHub account and viewing your reservations. You'll also receive email updates at each stage of your booking. For real-time assistance, contact our support team with your booking reference number.

Do you offer group bookings?

Yes! We offer special rates for group bookings. For groups of 10 or more people, please contact our group sales team at groups@travelhub.com or call +1 (555) 123-4567 for personalized assistance and competitive pricing.
